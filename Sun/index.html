Do you remember how many times you go onto NASA website to look for a rocket, a high resolution image of a star that is million light-years away?

This project is a research of statistic project where Lyra grabbed 10 source images from NASA that contains "Sun" in the span of 40 years. The goal is to find the sun in each collage image Lyra had made. Players also have the option to save the images, and light up the screen with the halo that is tracing the path of the mouse.
