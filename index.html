Lyra Xiong

I am an animator, web artist, and a 3D modeler.

Art is my way of rebellion. Being an artist for me means breaking the objectivity of physics; it also means challenging the authority of unjust political structure. My practice spans over different medias but with the same goal, of debating and defending against the information-feeding-tubes which contradict humanity.

In terms of content, my recent work focuses on translation across three dimensions, the 4D physical space we live in, the digital space where content lives “permanently” as data, and the intangible mental space one can never physically access.
